Korean Bulgogi

Ingredient Measurement
For marinade:
Pork Collar 500 grams
Soy Sauce 2 tbps
Apple, grated 1/2 apple
Sesame Oil 2 tsp
Mirin 2 tbps
Honey 2 tbps
Garlic 4 cloves
For vegetables:
Carrot 1
Zucchinni 1
Bean sprouts 4 cups

Instructions



1. Thinly slice pork collar. Put in freezer for 20 minutes and thinly cut if there is any difficulty.
2. In a large bowl, combine all marinade and let stand for 30 minutes to overnight.
3. Salt all vegetables and leave for 20 minutes, discard liquid afterwards
4. Boil or steam bean sprouts for 5 minutes, rinse with cold water and then set aside.
5. Stirfry all vegetables in a tbps of oil then remove.
6. Add tbsp of oil, fry pork until cooked. Allow the pork to caramelize.
